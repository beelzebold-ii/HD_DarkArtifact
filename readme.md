# HD_DarkArtifact

Loadout codes:

prc - dark artifact
 * level (0..13) - Customize the starting level of the artifact. This can also be done with the quantity instead.
 * tainted - If set, the bearer will spawn "tainted," without having to first equip the artifact.

#

### What does it do?

The dark artifact is mostly functionally the same as the Blursphere from older versions of HDest. Below is a detailed description.

When held by a player tainted by the dark artifact, it has the following effects:
 * Any blues within the bearer's body will react very flammably with the darkness. This severely damages both the artifact and its bearer. In the wrong circumstances, this can kill either party.
 * The artifact will scare off any Blurspheres immediately. It does not appreciate their presence.
 * The artifact will slowly passively gain XP. Every ~500 seconds with the artifact, it will gain a level. (With the caveat that levels are only gained when you equip the artifact.)
 * Every once and a while, the artifact may cause the bearer to hallucinate, or accrue aggravated damage. This is more frequent the higher the artifact's level.
 * The artifact will passively attempt to seal the bearer's wounds. This goes faster the higher the artifact's level. It may not do a very good job of sealing these wounds, and they may not fully heal.
 * Every now and again, the artifact may cause wide area effects to any nearby enemies. Effects can include mass immolation, mass bloodletting, and mass infighting. This grows stronger and more frequent the higher the artifact's level.
 * At its maximum level, the hallucinations, aggravated damage, and area effects only happen when the artifact is worn. At this point the bearer *has* to equip the artifact to get most of its positive effects, though thankfully this is also the case for aggravated damage.

When worn:
 * The artifact turns the user partially invisible. This appears to make enemies less likely to spot the player, but not incapable.
 * The artifact will gain XP 5x as fast, at a rate of one level every 100 seconds.
 * The artifact's occasional hallucinations or mass damage effects also happen in 5x faster intervals.
 * When gazing at an enemy, that enemy will begin to catch fire. The effect goes from very weak at base level to *very powerful* at its peak, able to bring down the strongest of demons with enough focus.
 * The artifact's worn effects will temporarily vanish if the bearer causes any bright lights of loud sounds, like from gunshots.

At higher levels, the bearer will find themselves unable to drop the artifact. By pressing  [ Zoom + Use (item) ] you will attempt to forcibly remove the artifact. This will HURT and cause lots of bleeding, so be ready to patch yourself up afterwards.

At its highest level, even this measure is no longer possible, so the wearer must burn off the darkness using blues. If they survive enough of the flame, the artifact will be damaged enough to be forcibly removed.

Sometimes, when faced with a substantial amount of healing magicks, the artifact will temporarily relinquish its hold on the bearer, untainting them. This will *not* weaken the artifact or allow the user to discard it without force, and in fact appears to be an attempt to prevent that from happening. However, it will prevent aggravated damage buildup and allow safe consumtion of blues. If you really wish to keep yourself tainted, simply wear the artifact.
